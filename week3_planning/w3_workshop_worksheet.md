### Question 3

*(Adapted from: June 2023 Examination, Question 1(b) — original: 6 marks)*

**Scenario:** Priya has recently been appointed as project manager for a major upgrade to a water treatment facility operated by a regional water utility in the East Midlands. The project involves installing new filtration technology and upgrading the site's control systems. Priya is currently producing her planning documents.

For **each** of the following planning documents, explain its purpose:

- Resource plan
- Financial plan
- Communication strategy

**(6 marks)**

*(2 marks per document: 1 mark for a correct statement of purpose, 1 mark for an explanation of why the document is important to the project)*

---

### Question 4

*(Adapted from: January 2022 Examination, Question 3(a) — original: 6 marks)*

**Scenario:** Marcus is the project manager overseeing the construction of a new automated assembly line at a UK aerospace components manufacturer. The total project cost is estimated at £2.4 million. The company's board has decided to fund the project using a bank loan (a form of debt finance).

Analyse **one advantage** and **one disadvantage** of using debt finance (a bank loan) to fund this project.

**(6 marks)**

*(For each of advantage and disadvantage: 1 mark for identification, 1 mark for explanation, 1 mark for analysis applied to the project context)*

### Question 5

**Scenario:** Aisha is a project manager at a UK rail engineering company. Her current project involves installing a new signalling system on a 40-kilometre stretch of track in the North West of England. Her project sponsor has asked her to write a clear project objective for the installation work.

Aisha writes the following draft objective:

> *"Install the new signalling system as quickly as possible and within budget."*

(a) Identify **two** ways in which Aisha's draft objective fails to meet the SMART criteria.
**(2 marks)**

(b) Rewrite Aisha's objective so that it meets **all five** SMART criteria. You may invent appropriate figures and details.
**(4 marks)**

*(1 mark for each SMART criterion clearly satisfied in the rewritten objective; maximum 4 marks if all five criteria are addressed)*

---

### Question 6

**Scenario:** Broadstone Engineering Ltd is planning a project to upgrade its manufacturing facility. The project manager, Dan, has been asked to produce a resource plan.

(a) State the **three categories** of resource that Dan should consider when producing the resource plan.
**(3 marks)**

(b) For **one** of the resource categories you have identified, give **two specific examples** of resources that Dan might need to plan for on an engineering manufacturing project.
**(2 marks)**

*(1 mark per correct example; examples must be specific, not generic)*

---

### Question 7

**Scenario:** A UK construction company is funding a new office development project with an estimated cost of £1.8 million. The finance director is preparing the financial plan.

(a) Explain the difference between **debt finance** and **equity finance** as sources of project funding.
**(4 marks)**

*(2 marks for a correct explanation of debt finance; 2 marks for a correct explanation of equity finance)*

(b) Identify **one internal** source of finance that the company could use for this project and explain **one** advantage of using an internal source.
**(2 marks)**
